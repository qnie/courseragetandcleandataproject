URL: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones

The features list at the bottom are summariZed in the tidy data set. The average values of the features were taken and grouped by the 6 activities and 30 subjects.

Some of the features have an X, Y or Z suffix, these indicate the 3-axial directions, where X is the left right directions, Y is the up down directions and Z is the forwards and backwards directions.

Only the mean and standard deviation for each of the features are included in the tidy data set.
